Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Data We Collect

We collect the following information to operate and improve the app:

  • Device Identifier: A unique device ID is generated to register your device with the Cloudflare WARP service. This ID is stored locally on your device.
  • Anonymous Analytics: We collect anonymous usage statistics such as app opens, connection events, and crash reports. These analytics do not identify you personally.

VPN Traffic Handling

When the VPN is active, your internet traffic is routed through Cloudflare's global network using the WARP protocol. The app itself does not log, inspect, or store any of your browsing activity or network traffic. Cloudflare's handling of your traffic is governed by Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.

Third-Party Services

This app uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Cloudflare WARP: Provides the VPN tunnel and network infrastructure. Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob: Used to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device information and advertising identifiers. Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Analytics: Used to collect anonymous usage data and crash reports. Privacy Policy

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Information is shared only with the third-party services listed above, solely for the purposes described in this policy.

Data Retention

Device registration data is stored locally on your device and can be cleared at any time from the app settings. Anonymous analytics data is retained by Firebase in accordance with their data retention policies.

Your Rights

You can clear your device registration from the app settings at any time. You may also uninstall the app to remove all locally stored data.
